Omen

Omen is the third Agent to join the VALORANT PROTOCOL. He uses his Radiant powers to move silently and stalk on his enemies. A phantom of a memory, Omen hunts in the shadows. He renders enemies blind, teleports across the field, then lets paranoia take hold as his foe scrambles to learn where he might strike next.

Omen is known for his mysterious and dark persona, often using quotes such as "Where will they run when the night closes in?" and "They will cower, they will tremble." in the game.

Brimstone

Brimstone is a playable character in the first-person shooter game Valorant developed by Riot Games. He is classified as a controller, and his abilities revolve around area denial and supportive utility. Brimstone is known for his military background and commanding persona, often using quotes such as "I've seen their tactics, I'll read them like a book" and "This old dog's learned a few tricks". Brimstone's signature ability is his Orbital Strike ultimate, which allows him to call in an airstrike that deals heavy damage to enemies in the designated area.

Reyna

Reyna is a playable character in the first-person shooter game Valorant developed by Riot Games. She is classified as a duelist, and her abilities revolve around gaining health, rapidly healing, and enhancing her abilities through defeating enemies. Reyna is known for her self-sufficient and aggressive playstyle, often using quotes such as "I am the hunter!" and "Enemy spotted. And they are dead." in the game. Reyna's signature ability is her Leer, which allows her to throw an ethereal eye that can blind enemies within its line of sight. If an enemy is killed while affected by her abilities, Reyna can consume their soul orb to gain health and temporary buffs to her abilities.

Raze

Raze is a playable character in the first-person shooter game Valorant developed by Riot Games. She is classified as a duelist, and her abilities revolve around dealing explosive damage and clearing out areas. Raze is known for her energetic and enthusiastic personality, often using quotes such as "Boom, baby!" and "I'm a whirlwind of fire and steel!" in the game. Raze's signature ability is her Blast Pack, which allows her to throw a satchel charge that can be remotely detonated, dealing damage to enemies and providing her with increased mobility. She also has a Paint Shells ability that allows her to fire a cluster grenade that deals damage in a small area, and a Showstopper ultimate ability that equips her with a rocket launcher that can deal massive damage to enemies.

Viper

Viper is a playable character in the first-person shooter game Valorant developed by Riot Games. She is classified as a controller, and her abilities revolve around creating and controlling poisonous gas to restrict enemy movements and vision. Viper is known for her stoic and calculating personality, often using quotes such as "My poison is your poison" and "I know exactly what to do". Viper's signature ability is her Toxic Screen, which allows her to create a line of gas that can be activated or deactivated at her will. She also has a Poison Cloud ability that allows her to throw a grenade that creates a large poisonous gas cloud, a Snake Bite ability that deals damage over time to enemies within its area.

Sova

Sova is a playable character in the first-person shooter game Valorant developed by Riot Games. He is classified as an initiator, and his abilities revolve around gathering information and scouting enemy locations. Sova is known for his calm and analytical personality, often using quotes such as "Trust in my recon" and "Let's make some chaos". Sova's signature ability is his Recon Bolt, which allows him to shoot an arrow that reveals the location of nearby enemies. He also has an Owl Drone ability that allows him to control a drone that can scout enemy positions and fire a dart that reveals enemies hit by it.
